Welcome to Chameleon!

This is a tutorial on how to play the game.

Roles:

  • Regular Players: Know the secret word and must give clues to help others guess the Chameleon.
  • Chameleon: Does not know the secret word and must blend in by giving convincing clues.

How to Play:

  1. Each player submits a one-word clue related to the secret word.
  2. Players then vote on who they think the Chameleon is.
  3. If the Chameleon is caught, they get a chance to guess the secret word.

Good luck, and have fun!
